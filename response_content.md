## 4. Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1: Cultural Exploration
- **9:00 AM** – Breakfast at [Grand Central Market](https://goo.gl/maps/XXY) (Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM, Cost: 100 HKD)
- **11:00 AM** – Visit [The Getty Center](https://goo.gl/maps/YYZ) for art and architecture (Free Entry, Parking: 90 HKD)
  - **Distance:** 10 km from Grand Central Market
  - **Travel Time:** ~30 minutes
- **2:00 PM** – Lunch at a local restaurant in [Westwood Village](https://goo.gl/maps/ZZZ) (Cost: 120 HKD)
- **4:00 PM** – Explore [Santa Monica Pier](https://goo.gl/maps/AAA) (Opening Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM, Free entry)
  - **Distance to Santa Monica Pier:** 15 km
  - **Travel Time:** ~40 minutes
- **7:00 PM** – Dinner & Sunset at [Venice Beach Boardwalk](https://goo.gl/maps/BBB) (200 HKD)

### Day 2: Shopping and Nightlife
- **10:00 AM** – Retail therapy at [The Grove](https://goo.gl/maps/CCC) (Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM)
- **1:00 PM** – Have lunch at [Farmer’s Market](https://goo.gl/maps/DDD) near The Grove (Cost: 150 HKD)
- **3:00 PM** – Explore Hidden Gems in [Silverlake](https://goo.gl/maps/EEE)
  - **Travel:** 5 km from The Grove
  - **Time:** 15 minutes drive
- **7:00 PM** – Dinner and Night Out in [Hollywood](https://goo.gl/maps/FFF), visit various bars (300 HKD)

### Day 3: Nature and Relaxation
- **8:00 AM** – Breakfast at nearby café near accommodation (Cost: 70 HKD)
- **10:00 AM** – Hike at [Griffith Park and Observatory](https://goo.gl/maps/GGG) (Recommend the morning for the best views and cooler temperatures).
  - **Entrance Fee:** Free
  - **Parking:** 50 HKD
- **2:00 PM** – Lunch at [Los Feliz neighborhood](https://goo.gl/maps/HHH) (Cost: 150 HKD)
- **5:00 PM** – Chill at [Echo Park Lake](https://goo.gl/maps/III)
  - **Distance from Griffith Park:** 8 km
  - **Travel Time:** ~20 minutes
  - **Rent pedal boats:** 100 HKD (1 hour)
- **7:00 PM** – Dinner at a local Mexican restaurant [Guelaguetza](https://goo.gl/maps/JJJ) (Cost: 200 HKD)
