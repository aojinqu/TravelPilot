
## Trip Overview

**Destination:** Chongqing, China  
**Duration:** 3 days (December 3 - December 6, 2025)  
**Budget:** 2000 HKD total  
**Preferences:** Cultural attractions, local food experiences, scenic views  

### Main Scenic Spots
- **Jiefangbei CBD**: A bustling area known for shopping and its iconic monument.
- **Chongqing People's Hall**: Famous for its architectural beauty and cultural significance.
- **Hongya Cave**: A traditional-style building complex offering beautiful views, food, and cultural experiences.
- **Three Gorges Museum**: A museum showcasing the history and artifacts of the region.
- **Dazu Rock Carvings**: UNESCO World Heritage site known for its intricate Buddhist sculptures.

## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival and Sightseeing
- **9:00 AM - Arrival at Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport**
  - **Transportation:** Taxi to accommodation ($50 HKD)
  - **Travel Time:** 30 mins
  
- **10:00 AM - Check-in to Airbnb**
  - **Location:** Based on chosen Airbnb option

- **11:00 AM - Visit Jiefangbei CBD**
  - **Description:** Explore the vibrant shopping street with local eateries and the iconic liberation monument.
  - **Cost:** Free
  - **Distance:** 1 km from Airbnb
  - **Travel Time:** 15 min walk
  
- **1:00 PM - Lunch at Local Restaurant**
  - **Location:** Lao Si Chuan Hot Pot, Jiejiang Lu, Chongqing
  - **Cost:** $100 HKD
  
- **3:00 PM - Visit Chongqing People's Hall**
  - **Location:** Renmin Road, Yuzhong District
  - **Cost:** $20 HKD
  - **Opening Hours:** 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  - **Distance:** 3 km from Jiefangbei
  - **Travel Time:** 10 min Taxi ($15 HKD)

- **6:00 PM - Dinner and Explore Hongya Cave**
  - **Location:** Hongya Cave, Jialing River
  - **Description:** Dine with a view and explore the cultural shops and beautiful night views.
  - **Cost:** $150 HKD
  - **Distance:** 2.5 km from Chongqing People's Hall
  - **Travel Time:** 8 min Taxi ($10 HKD)
